My history

Back in 2013 I adopted an interesting hobby apart from programming i.e. blogging. Started my first blog in late 2013 which was hosted on Blogspot dedicated to helping other with tips, tricks and simple computer programs.

ManyCode was started at the beginning of 2017. At ManyCode, we all come to work everyday because we want to help other with our softwares. I started ManyCode as GitHub’s organization when I realized there are no software (atleast not for free) available for several small problems. We work on small as well as large open-source projects.

An example of one of our greatest projects DUKES (Dual Key Encryption System) which combines two strong ciphers to build a very strong encryption system which is practically impossible to break.

Most of our projects are designed in Python but we also work with other languages like C, C++, and Java.

You are always welcome to join us @ our GitHub organization. If you want to know more about us feel free to contact us. We will love to hear from you.
